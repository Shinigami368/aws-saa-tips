Why I Built This

I built this while preparing for the AWS SAA-C03 exam. I wanted a free, practical resource where I could solve realistic questions and jump from each question to the relevant AWS documentation, but I could not find one that fit exactly how I wanted to study.

At first, I planned to share my own notes. While organizing them, I decided to go further and turn the material into an interactive quiz bank. After several focused days of writing, reviewing, reducing the scope, and checking the content repeatedly, the result is this open study resource: 325 original questions written from scratch, with explanations, key rules, and official AWS documentation links.

If you find a clear technical mistake, broken link, ambiguous question, UI issue, or any content ownership, copyright, or publication concern, please contact me at cakibey368@gmail.com. I want to cooperate on legitimate issues and keep this resource useful, independent, and safe for people who are studying.
